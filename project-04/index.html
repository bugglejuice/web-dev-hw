WELCOME to the humble home of RoboFungus. We are a multimedium studio who work on digital paintings, intricate animatronic cosplay, and our pride, joy and labor of love, video games. We are attempting to shift gears and become a full blown game development studio. We can't disclose any of our projects at the moment but we are gearing up to start a podcast and a youtube channel where we plan to update weekly on all of our ongoing projects. So stay tuned for that!

You may have noticed the little guy on top of the page. That's our logo and mascot, Fergus the fungus crusader. It's a bit of a funny story how he came to be actually, While making this site, delerious from lack of sleep, I knew what I wanted the Logo to look like and I drew him in about 20 minutes. Oh the things you can do when exhausted.
